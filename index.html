MercadoPago

Test Exercise

When making a payment by credit card, you must specify the amount, the means of payment, the bank and the amount of fees, plus a secure identification (token) of the card with which payment is being made .
The sequence of screens must be: 1. Amount entry screen. 2. Selection of means of payment (type credit_card). The means of payment can be: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, etc. Show name and image To access these means of payment, consult the following URL: https://api.mercadopago.com/v1/payment_methods?public_key=PUBLIC_KEY (GET Method). 3. Bank selection. The banks can be: ICBC, Santander, Galicia, among others. Show name and image These operate with different payment means of payment, so you must consume an API to be able to know which banks are available for the selected means of payment. https://api.mercadopago.com/v1/payment_methods/card_issuers?public_key=PUBLIC_KEY&payment_method_id=MEDIO_DE_PAGO_SELECCIONADO (GET Method). 4. Quota selection. After having the means of payment and the bank selected, the user must select the amount of installments in which he wishes to pay the amount entered in step 1. The API provides a recommended_message that resolves the message that must be shown to the user with the amount of installments, the value of each installment and the final amount. Show the recommended_message. https://api.mercadopago.com/v1/payment_methods/installments?public_key=PUBLIC_KEY&amount=MONTO_DEL_PASO_1&payment_method_id =MEDIO_DE_PAGO_SELECTED&issuer.id=ISSUER_SELECTED (GET Method).

  • Date: 2018
  • Client: Personal Client
  • Category: Android Application
  • Programming Language: Android/Java (Android Studio)
